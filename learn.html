Learn

Harmony Engine was designed with simplicity in mind to allow anyone to jump right in and start creating with little to no learning curve.

Choose Method

Discord via. Patreon

Learn from real experienced people by joining our team to get access to the discord server. Not to mention the extra features patrons get.

Getting Started Docs.

Get started with Harmony Engine by reading the getting started documentation. It will show you how to get off the ground from a technical stand-point.

Jump Right In

Sometimes the best way to learn is just to jump right into it. Go and download Harmony Engine to jump right in and get your feet wet right away.

Easy to Learn

Harmony Engine was designed to be easy to learn and have a small learning-curve that makes it easy for anyone to get started with.

Easy to Integrate into the Classroom

Harmony Engine is easy to implement into the classroom thanks to its easy and beginner-friendly design. Educators can use Harmony Engine to teach about computer science in the classroom.

Works Well with Existing Technologies

Harmony Engine with it's XML data structure is set-up in just the right way to work with most version control technologies making it feel right at home.
