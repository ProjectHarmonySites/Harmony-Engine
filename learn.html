Jump Right In

Sometimes the best way to learn is just to jump right into it. Go and download Harmony Engine to jump right in and get your feet wet right away.

Easy To Learn

Harmony Engine was designed to be easy to learn and have a small learning-curve that makes it easy for anyone to get started with.



Easy to integrate into the classroom

Harmony Engine is easy to implement into the classroom thanks to it's easy and beginner-friendly design. Educators can use Harmony Engine to teach about computer science in the classroom.



Works with existing technologies


Harmony Engine with it's XML data structure is set-up in just the right way to work with most version control technologies making it feel right at home.
